Mysterious Collapse of the Usher Mansion

ElAbueloKraken | Le Cinema Paradiso

Breaking News:
Last night, there was a sudden collapse of the House of Usher. Neighbors say they knew "there was something weird about that house". The owners of the mansion, Roderick and Madeline Usher, were pronounced dead at the scene. Roderick's friend, whose name is currently unknown, states that Roderick and Madeline were sick with a mental disease. He also says that they buried Madeline because, and I quote, "She was dead one day, and then she haunted us the next day." He also states that Madeline appeared in a ghost-like figure and when Roderick saw her, he died of fear. The old, cracked, spooky mansion came crumbling down just seconds after Roderick's friend made it out of the house. Neighbors say, "It randomly started storming, and then, *BOOM* and I saw when the house came crashing down." Police are still investigating the scene to determine what could have caused this spooky event. More on this story when we get more information.
